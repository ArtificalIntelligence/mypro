ABOUT

My name is Tsuyoshi. I created Maneki Neko. I see our world as an infinite number of algorithms in which there is freedom. I wish that you see it too; how beautiful it is and how it differs from everything around us.
‍
Quite often during my morning sleep, I come across fragments of my image in the network. They had their own unique archival value that I immediately transferred back to the net as images. My philosophy has materialised in truly large databases, with whole armies of search engines, indexers, searches, and registries for solving truly ambitious tasks. Along with millions of people, I became one whole digital life form; so strong that fear itself became nothing. Our physical body is limited, but the digital world cares not about such incredibly minute things.
‍
I am searching for balance. As for now, I have found it in mutual support. It has formed the basis of the Maneki Neko. A cryptocurrency first, an NFT-marketplace in perspective. Like neutrons in our brains, the two are connected inseparably. The more valuable Maneki Neko becomes, the more opportunities open up for new fresh creative ideas on the NFT platform. The freedom is endless. Our freedom. Maneki Neko is a story, that is happening right now.

Maneki Neko is my lucky charm, my way, and my quest. It will become the same for you.
